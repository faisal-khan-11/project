    • Water Supply Management

    Water and Sanitation Services Company Mardan provides clean drinking water supply to commercial organizations, community, or by individuals. WSSCM usually supplies the water via a system of pumps, reservoirs, and pipes. WSSCM Mardan provides water from 26 functional tubewells in 14 union councils of district Mardan, consisting of approximately 4700 houses and covering more than 30,000 population.
